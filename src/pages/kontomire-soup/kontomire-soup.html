KONTOMIRE SOUP

INGREDIENTS

1.Kontomire (Spinach)

2.Peanut butter

3.Salted tilapia

3.Salted tilapia

5.Tomatoes

6.Onion

7.Palm oil

8.Eggs

9.Avocado

10.Plantain

11.Salt

1.How to prepare kontomire stew /palava sauce: Directions

2.Wash kontomire and pepper. Boil afterwards

3.Wash kontomire and pepper. Boil afterwards

4.Wash all vegetables

5.Peel plantain and boil with enough water.

6.Add the eggs to the boiling plantain.

7.Chop onion and tomatoes

8.When the kontomire and pepper are soft, drain the water and grind in asanka

9.Add half onion and a teaspoon of peanut butter to the kontomire and grind

10.Heat palm oil in a saucepan, add onion and allow to simmer.

11.When the edges of the onion of brown, add the salted tilapia.

12.After 3-5 minutes, add the tomatoes and stir continuously.

13.Turn off the heat and put the content onto the kontomire.

14.Check if the plantains are soft, remove the eggs. Then add water and allow to boil

15.Remove the eggs shells, wash and add the kontomire.

>16.Drain off all the water from the plantain and serve.

17.Garnish the kontomire stew with avocado and serve
